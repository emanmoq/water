who are we

Water Experts Office for Water Studies and Consultations

The Water Experts Office for Water Studies and Consultations was established in 1434H (2013). It is a consultant office for conducting geological, hydrological and hydrogeological studies. The work of the office is supervised by a team specialized in the world of water and geology under the supervision of Dr. Ali bin Saad Al-Tokhis.

And then the Ministry of Water and Electricity until he retired from the post of Undersecretary of the Ministry of Water and Electricity in 1434 after spending more than 30 years in the field of water in the government apparatus, and holds a doctorate from the University of Colorado (Fort Collins) (Groundwater Management Strategies in Saudi Arabia), Master's Degree from Ohio State University (Athens), USA, and Thesis Title (Quantitative Study of Drinking Water Field in Wasea, East of Riyadh) Water experts for water studies and consultations, which, God willing, is developing day by day until its work covers all aspects of groundwater. Design of technical specifications for all pipe and manual wells in the Kingdom / Drilling of wells and supervision of drilling / (Video camera / maintenance and solution of wells problems), as well as assistance in research and studies concerned with groundwater and methods of rationalization of exploitation and conservation. The Office of Water Experts is currently working as a consultant for the Directorate of Water in Riyadh for three years. The office also includes a specialized group of full-time scientific staff who are distinguished by their vast and extensive expertise in the various fields of work including all branches of groundwater, geophysics and well drilling. And the work carried out by the Office with the highest levels of accuracy and professionalism.
